Academics

The scholastic and co scholastic domains identified by CBSE are religiously followed at One World Junior High and define our institution qualitatively. They make for an education that is holistic and comprehensively assesses an individual’s potential, capabilities and interests

Scholastic


We at One World aim at the 21st curriculum reducing content and focusing on conceptual understanding. Students are encouraged to develop deeper understanding and are provided with opportunities for self discovery and self learning. Learners here are challenged to think, reflect, internalize and express. They are encouraged to take charge of their own learning, engage readily with activities, ask questions and use technology. Regular and periodic assessment in the form of Formative and Summative using a variety of tools and techniques help in identifying the levels of attainment.


Co-Scholastic


One World Junior High offers a plethora of activities encompassing Art, Craft, Music, Dance (ACMD) classes. This aims to strike a perfect balance between the Indian and western culture. ACMD classes are designed in such a way that it gives every student an opportunity to learn beyond studies. We believe that these activities can bring social & intellectual skills, moral, cultural & ethical values, personality development, and character advancement in students. ACMD classes are a part of the curriculum from classes VI to X and are held once weekly for every class.
